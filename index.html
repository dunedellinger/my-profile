My Favorite Places

As I mentionned above one of my favorite activities is travelling. For you to get to know me better, here are my Top 3 favorite places on earth!

COSTA RICA

One of my favorite trips was to Costa Rica in 2021. The beautiful natural environment combined with the high presence of wild creatures respected by the locals truly make this a paradise on earth. On this trip, forget about the cell phone and luxury 5 star and be ready for late night surfing with sharks and sunrise sightseeing!

DUBAI

An unforgettable city to visit has to be Dubai! With the numerous skyscrappers and the extensive immersion in complete luxury, Dubai has to be everyone's dream vacation. Between the fancy high sky dinners and the camel rides in the desert; Dubai is a once in a lifetime "must see".

CUBA

Despite their difficult political context, the Cubans have to be the sweatest people on earth always ready to wear their heart on their sleeves. The vintage infrastructures in Havana are one of a kind which I promise will make this trip truly breathtaking.
